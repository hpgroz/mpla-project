CLICK ON NUTTY OR FLIPPY TO START THE GAME!


Navigate the Happy Tree Friends characters collecting candies to increase your score while avoiding the empty candy wraps.

The Player with more points at the end wins! The one who grabs the empty candy wrap loses!

Candies are worth 1 point, lolipops increases score by 5 points, while sugar canes boost you score by 10 points!

Player 1: use the left, right, up, down arrow keys to control Nutty on the righthand side!

Player 2: use A, D, W, S keyboard keys to control Flippy on the lefthand side!
